STFLRMAT is the ST Floormat Demo by the Senior Dads.  
This demo will run on the ST-Falcon computer with a color monitor.  It seems that this group had a bit of an argument with the ST Floormat magazine. Apparently the magazine back in 1984(?, but that's what the docs say) took their demo and used it without giving them credit.  Now that ST Floormat is no more, they are releasing it for themselves.  Now, it really isn't all that wild a demo (perhaps though for being so old it is).  It shows some screens of text and a scolling text moving over the top of the screen.
